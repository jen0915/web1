빈야사요가

Vinyāsa (/vɪnˈjɑːsə/ vin-YAH-sə; Sanskrit: विन्यास, IAST: vinyāsa) is a Sanskrit term often employed in relation to certain styles of yoga, that usually refers to a transition between two different positions.[1] Like many Sanskrit words, vinyāsa has many meanings; nyasa denotes "to place" and vi denotes "in a special way". The term "Vinyāsa" can also be used to reference a style of yoga practiced as one breath, tied to one movement.
